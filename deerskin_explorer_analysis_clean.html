Note the direction: SZ < HC. The schizophrenic temporal field has fewer topological loops, not more. With ICA preprocessing active, the "hyper-geometric" prediction of the paper reverses.

The ICA Question Remains
The paper's geometric_dysrhythmia.py found SZ > HC without ICA. The Explorer finds SZ < HC with ICA. This isn't a contradiction — ICA removes non-neural artifacts that inflate Betti-1 asymmetrically across groups. The key experiment: run geometric_dysrhythmia.py both with and without ICA on the same data to see where the flip happens.

VIII. What Stays from the Paper, What Changes

Confirmed

The geometric measurement framework works. Takens embedding + persistence homology + cross-band coupling + PLV produce real discriminative signal from clinical EEG without any ML. Cross-band coupling alone achieves 80.8% accuracy (21/26 correct) with a threshold classifier. The three layers are statistically independent (all cross-correlations NS), confirming they capture distinct geometric aspects of the field.

Revised

Direction of coupling: SZ shows reduced, not elevated, cross-band coupling. The "lockstep" interpretation needs to become "fragmentation." The paper's eigenmode vocabulary analysis (dominant mode index correlation) may still show the opposite pattern — resolving this is the single most important next experiment.

Direction of Betti-1: With ICA, SZ shows reduced temporal complexity. The "hyper-geometric" label needs either qualification ("hyper-geometric before artifact removal") or revision.

Gate narrative: "Hijacked" → "unstable." The theta gate isn't locked rigid on hallucinated content — it's intermittent, failing to maintain stable temporal organisation.

Next Experiments

1. Run geometric_dysrhythmia.py ± ICA to resolve the Betti-1 direction question.

2. Implement eigenmode vocabulary coupling in the Explorer to compare with power correlation coupling.

3. Add pairwise multi-channel PLV to the Explorer for proper gate assessment.

4. Add automatic data-quality exclusion (coupling > 0.95 or min Betti < 5.0) to the Explorer's batch analysis pipeline.
